Meet Dr. Andrew

Dr. Andrew Trinh is a native San Franciscan and a graduate of Lowell High School. He attended University of California, Riverside and received his Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry before receiving his D.D.S degree from the University of the Pacific Arthur A. Dugoni School of Dentistry. After finishing dental school, he completed his educational career at the exceptional residency program in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery at LSU Health Shreveport. Dr. Andrew continued his specialty training at University of Miami where he experienced a high volume of skull base surgery and microvascular reconstructive surgery. He remains hungry for more in his education by staying updated with improved methods for reconstructing trauma or pathology related jaw injuries at annual American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons (AAOMS) conferences.

Dr. Andrew takes part in many professional affiliations including the American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons, Western Society of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, California Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, Academy of Osseointegration, American Dental Association, California Dental Association, and San Francisco Dental Society. He has served over ten years of dental care to local communities in the Bay Area. Year after year, he continues to volunteer his time in various committees and boards of directors in the many projects he takes on.

Dr. Andrew competes in tennis and basketball tournaments, while rooting on the Golden State Warriors, San Francisco 49ers, and San Francisco Giants. He enjoys spending precious time with his family on hikes, vacation, and movie nights. Eating authentic Vietnamese food is one of his favorite passions, in addition to catching up with his friends and family.
